# Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) Policy — Training Outcomes

Source: [CUI Policy](../policies/621641798.md)

| Policy | Section | Learning Outcome | Audience |
|--------|---------|-----------------|----------|
| CUI | Overview | Define CUI and give examples relevant to CivicActions (vulnerability scan results, contractor bids/proposals, non-public contract data); explain the need-to-know principle for CUI access | All Staff working with CUI |
| CUI | Safeguarding CUI | Take steps to prevent unauthorized access to or observation of CUI; protect CUI confidentiality on federal and limited-access CivicActions systems; contact CISO/CTO or #loving-security if requirements are unclear | All Staff working with CUI |
| CUI | CUI Handling Guidelines | Do not transfer, print, or save CUI outside the CivicActions CUI Security Boundary; delete copies as soon as no longer needed; include CUI audit in project closeout procedures; report suspected compromise as a security incident | All Staff working with CUI |
| CUI | CUI Security Boundary | Identify the three approved CUI locations: secured client network, CivicActions Google Workspace (access-controlled Shared Drives marked "CUI"), and CivicActions Workstations (managed device or approved hardened BYOD); understand that Google Workspace auto-tags documents containing "CUI" | All Staff working with CUI |
| CUI | CUI Document Markings | Apply correct CUI markings when sharing outside a security boundary: "CONTROLLED UNCLASSIFIED INFORMATION" or "CUI" in the header, "Sensitive in accordance with 32 CFR 2002" centered at bottom of first page, and optional originator line per agency | All Staff working with CUI |
