# Physical Security Policy — Training Outcomes

Source: [Physical Security Policy](../policies/Physical-Security-Policy_745209857.md)

| Policy | Section | Learning Outcome | Audience |
|--------|---------|-----------------|----------|
| PSP | Overview and Principles | Explain CivicActions' remote-first approach to physical security: managed devices, encryption, and simple habits replace costly physical controls; understand that baselines are enforced by technology (FDE, auto-lock, remote wipe) and supplemented by personal habits | All Staff |
| PSP | Home Workspace Minimums | Apply the four home workspace must-dos: control screen visibility, secure printed material (shred same-day), keep devices under your control, and protect devices from environmental hazards (spills, heat, fire); use a surge-protected outlet | All Staff |
| PSP | Networks and Wi-Fi at Home | Use encrypted Wi-Fi (WPA2/WPA3) or wired Ethernet with a non-default router admin password; do not bypass TLS warnings; use a personal hotspot if secure Wi-Fi is unavailable | All Staff |
| PSP | Visitors and Working Around Others | Avoid sensitive conversations when unauthorized people are present; use a headset for calls; lock device and put sensitive papers away when non-household members are in your work area | All Staff |
| PSP | Travel and Public Locations | Keep devices with you or in a physically secure location; manually lock screen when away; avoid screen visibility of sensitive info; use encrypted Wi-Fi or personal hotspot (never public Wi-Fi); do not print sensitive material while traveling unless absolutely required | All Staff |
| PSP | Paper, Removable Media, and End-of-Life | Avoid printing and removable media by default; if printed, secure and shred same-day; encrypt removable media if used; follow CivicActions remote wipe and ITAD processes with chain-of-custody and certificates for device sanitization/disposal | All Staff |
| PSP | Lost, Stolen, or Physically Compromised Assets | Report immediately via Incident Response channels (what, when, where, which asset, suspected exposure); understand this applies to both CivicActions laptops and personal devices that authenticated to CivicActions systems; reporting is blame-free | All Staff |
| PSP | Future Facilities and Client Spaces | Understand that if CivicActions establishes a facility or operates in a client space, physical access controls, visitor logs, environmental protections, and quarterly access reviews will apply | All Staff |
| PSP | Evidence, Monitoring, and Continual Improvement | Understand that MDM posture, remote wipe records, incident reports, and ITAD certificates serve as audit evidence; key metrics include time-to-report lost assets, successful remote wipes, and destruction record closure | IT / Service Desk; System/Data Owners |
